Headline performance statistics

User story

"As a Welsh tax payer, I need to know how well the Welsh Revenue Authority are performing against their objectives, so that I can be confident in using their services."

Solution

Objectives are presented as headings.

Statistical performance against each objective is stated under each heading.

Language is simplified and commentary brief so that users can review performance at a glance.

Example

Welsh Revenue Authority service performance report 2022-23

Ease of using our service

91% of people found it easy to use our services

Supporting people to get their taxes right

98% of transactions were correct first time

Reducing the scope for tax risks

The number of tax risk cases decreased during this financial year. There was a large drop in tax risk cases from the end of the previous year.

Filing tax returns on time

99% of tax returns were filed on time. This is above our set target of 98%

Paying taxes on time

95% of tax returns were paid on time. A small increase from last year.

Issuing refunds on time

We met our objective of processing 95% of cases within 30 days. This equalled our performance in the previous 2 financial years. We processed 99% of cases within 60 days, falling just short of our objective of 100%.

Handling debt

We met our objective of paying off 90% of debts within 30 days. Debtors paid 96% of of transactions within 90 days, falling just short of our objective of 98%.

Correct payments first time

94% of payments were made correctly first time. This is an increase over the last two financial years.
